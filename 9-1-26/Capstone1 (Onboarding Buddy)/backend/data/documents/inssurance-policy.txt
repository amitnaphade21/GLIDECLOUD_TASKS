GlideCloud Employee Health Insurance Policy

GlideCloud Technologies provides health insurance coverage to all eligible employees to ensure financial protection during medical emergencies.

1. Objective

The objective of this policy is to provide medical insurance coverage to employees and their dependents in order to reduce financial stress during hospitalization or major medical treatment.

2. Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their probation period are eligible for health insurance coverage under the company group health insurance plan.

3. Coverage Amount

Each employee is covered for a sum insured of INR 5,00,000 (Five Lakhs) per year. This coverage also includes dependents such as spouse and up to two children.

4. Inclusions

The insurance policy covers the following:
- Hospitalization expenses
- Pre and post hospitalization expenses
- Day-care procedures
- Ambulance charges
- Certain critical illnesses as defined by the insurance provider

5. Exclusions

The policy does not cover:
- Cosmetic surgeries
- Non-prescribed treatments
- Injuries due to self-harm
- Treatment for pre-existing diseases during the initial waiting period

6. Claim Process

In case of hospitalization, the employee must inform the HR department as soon as possible. Cashless treatment can be availed at network hospitals. For reimbursement claims, all original bills and documents must be submitted to HR within 15 days of discharge.

7. Policy Renewal

The insurance policy is renewed annually by the company. Employees will be informed in advance in case of any changes to coverage or benefits.

8. Termination of Coverage

Insurance coverage will cease on the last working day of the employee with the company.

9. Amendments

GlideCloud reserves the right to modify or discontinue this benefit based on business requirements or insurance provider policies.
