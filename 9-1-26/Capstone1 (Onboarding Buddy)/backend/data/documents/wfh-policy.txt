GlideCloud Work From Home (WFH) Policy

This policy defines the guidelines and rules for employees who wish to work from home either temporarily or permanently.

1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide flexibility to employees while ensuring productivity, data security, and business continuity.

2. Eligibility

Work from home is allowed for employees based on the nature of their role, performance history, and approval from their reporting manager.

3. Types of Work From Home

- Occasional WFH: For short-term needs such as personal commitments.
- Long-term WFH: For employees working remotely for extended periods.

4. Working Hours

Employees working from home must be available during standard office hours from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM unless otherwise agreed.

5. Productivity Expectations

Employees are expected to maintain the same level of productivity and responsiveness as if they were working from the office.

6. Data Security

Employees must ensure:
- Company data is not shared with unauthorized persons
- Office systems are accessed securely
- Personal devices are protected with passwords and antivirus software

7. Communication

Employees must attend all scheduled meetings and be reachable via official communication channels such as email, Slack, or Microsoft Teams.

8. Equipment

The company may provide laptops or other required equipment based on role and availability.

9. Revocation

The company reserves the right to revoke WFH privileges if performance or discipline issues are observed.

10. Policy Violation

Violation of this policy may lead to disciplinary action.
